# Gluster卷状态

*gluster\_volume\_states*元素罗列出了Gluster卷可用状态。

             
    <capabilities>
    <version major="3" minor="3">
    ...
    <gluster_volume_states>
    <state>up</state>
    <state>down</state>
    </gluster_volume_states>
    ...
    </version>
    </capabilities>
